The Best Movies According to Muhammad Furrukh

My top 3 movies, anime and anime series of all time.

Hajime no Ippo

Best boxing series. Full of action and comedy.

Ben 10

Childhood memories and has cool artstyle with super cool aliens and powers. I love all the series from classic to ultimate alien. Omniverse artstyle is a liitle goofy but very intereting story telling. Hate the reboot!

Naruto

Literally the first anime I ever watched. Has cool OP, fun, comedic and wholesome moments. It also taught a lot of important life lessons to me.
